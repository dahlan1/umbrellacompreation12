Nemesis

Code Name: Nemesis T-Type
Height: 7'2" (2.2m)
Weight: 635 lbs (288kg)
Primary Weapon: Rocket Launcher
Intelligence: High (Can use weapons)
Threat Level: Extreme

Nemesis is a genetically enhanced bio-weapon created by Umbrella Corporation. Designed as an unstoppable killing machine, it can learn and adapt during combat. It relentlessly hunts S.T.A.R.S. members and is one of the deadliest foes in the universe.

Mr. X (Tyrant T-002)

Code Name: T-00
Height: 6'5" (1.96m)
Weight: 330 lbs (150kg)
Primary Weapon: None (Brute Strength)
Intelligence: Basic (Follows simple commands)
Threat Level: High

Mr. X, also known as Tyrant T-002, is a bio-organic weapon designed to eliminate survivors and retrieve critical data during the Raccoon City incident. It is known for its imposing size, relentless pursuit, and deadly combat capabilities.

Leadership & Founders

Oswell E. Spencer

CEO & Co-Founder

The aristocratic mastermind behind Umbrella Corporation. Founded the company in 1968 to pursue immortality through viral research. His vision of a new world order through eugenics drove Umbrella's darkest experiments. Died in 2006 during a power struggle with Albert Wesker.

James Marcus

Co-Founder & Lead Researcher

Pioneer of the T-virus research. Mentored William Birkin and Albert Wesker. Assassinated in 1988 but later resurrected through leech DNA fusion. Led the Arklay Laboratory outbreak before being eliminated by Rebecca Chambers and Billy Coen.

Edward Ashford

Co-Founder

Descendant of the aristocratic Ashford family. His genetic research laid the foundation for Umbrella's early work. Died in 1968 shortly after the company's founding, with his contributions largely overshadowed by Spencer's later work.

Chief Researchers

William Birkin

Chief Researcher (G-Virus)

Lead developer of the G-virus. Worked closely with Albert Wesker in Raccoon City. Betrayed by Umbrella and mortally wounded by HUNK's team in 1998. Injected himself with G-virus and transformed into a monstrous creature before being eliminated in the sewers beneath Raccoon City.

Albert Wesker

Chief Researcher (T-Virus)

Former S.T.A.R.S. captain turned Umbrella researcher. Infected himself with a prototype virus that granted superhuman abilities. Orchestrated multiple outbreaks to gather combat data. Presumed dead in 2009 after a confrontation with Chris Redfield and Sheva Alomar in Africa.

Alexia Ashford

Lead Researcher (T-Veronica)

Child prodigy who developed the T-Veronica virus at age 10. Placed herself in cryogenic sleep for 15 years to synchronize with the virus. Woke in 2002 with god-like powers before being defeated by Chris Redfield and Claire Redfield on Rockfort Island.

Security Division

HUNK

Umbrella Security Service (USS) Leader

Nicknamed "The Grim Reaper" for his survival skills. Led the team that recovered the G-virus from William Birkin. Sole survivor of multiple extraction missions. Current status unknown following Umbrella's collapse.

Nikolai Zinoviev

UBCS Mercenary

Umbrella Biohazard Countermeasure Service operative. Secretly gathering B.O.W. combat data during Raccoon City outbreak. Betrayed his team multiple times for profit. Escaped Raccoon City before its destruction.

Sergei Vladimir

Umbrella Security Service Commander

Former Soviet special forces officer. Led Umbrella's private army and Tyrant development program. Cloned himself multiple times. Killed during the raid on Umbrella's Antarctic Base in 2003.
